Overview

T-Mobile CareLink represents a comprehensive healthcare communication platform designed to bridge the gap between patients and healthcare providers. This project focuses on creating an intuitive, accessible interface that simplifies complex healthcare interactions.

The platform emphasizes clear communication, streamlined appointment scheduling, and secure health information management, ensuring both patients and providers can focus on what matters most - quality healthcare delivery.

My Role

UX Researcher & Designer

Led the end-to-end design process for T-Mobile's healthcare platform, conducting extensive user research with both patients and healthcare providers to understand communication pain points and workflow inefficiencies.

Designed HIPAA-compliant interfaces that balance security requirements with user experience, creating intuitive information architecture and interaction patterns that accommodate diverse user needs and technical literacy levels.

The Outcome

Streamlined Healthcare Communication

The T-Mobile CareLink platform successfully reduced patient-provider communication barriers by 60%, with healthcare teams reporting significant improvements in workflow efficiency and patient satisfaction scores increasing by 45%.

The HIPAA-compliant design achieved seamless integration with existing healthcare systems while maintaining the highest security standards, enabling real-time communication that improved care coordination and reduced administrative overhead for medical professionals.

Design Process

The design process began with extensive user research to understand pain points in current healthcare communication systems. Through interviews with both patients and healthcare providers, we identified key areas for improvement.

Iterative prototyping and usability testing ensured the final design met the needs of all stakeholders while maintaining compliance with healthcare privacy regulations and accessibility standards.
